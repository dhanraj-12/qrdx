## Preventing Abuse

qrdx is committed to preventing abuse and misuse of its platform to create malicious QR codes, including but not limited to the following:

- Phishing or scam websites
- Pornography or adult content
- Betting or gambling
- Copyright infringement
- Malware distribution
- Hate speech
- Illegal goods and services
- Terrorism or violent extremism

However, since QR codes are created by individual users, we are unable to control the content of every QR code created on our platform.

## Report a QR Code

If you believe a QR code created with qrdx is being used for malicious purposes, please report it to us by emailing [support@qrdx.dev](mailto:support@qrdx.dev) with the following information:

- **Your Name:** (Optional but helpful)
- **Your Email:** So we can follow up with you
- **QR Code Details:** URL or screenshot of the problematic QR code
- **Type of Abuse:** What kind of malicious content it links to
- **Reason for Reporting:** Detailed description of why you're reporting this QR code

## Our Response

We take all abuse reports seriously and will:

1. Review your report within 24-48 hours
2. Investigate the reported QR code
3. Take appropriate action if the QR code violates our Terms of Service
4. Follow up with you via email regarding the outcome

## Types of Abuse We Monitor

### Phishing & Scams
QR codes linking to fake login pages, fraudulent websites, or scam operations designed to steal personal information.

### Malware Distribution
QR codes that redirect to websites hosting or distributing malware, viruses, or other malicious software.

### Adult Content
QR codes linking to pornographic or sexually explicit material, especially when not properly labeled or targeted at minors.

### Illegal Content
QR codes promoting illegal activities, controlled substances, weapons, or other prohibited goods and services.

### Copyright Infringement
QR codes used to distribute pirated content, counterfeit goods, or materials that violate intellectual property rights.

### Hate Speech
QR codes linking to content promoting violence, discrimination, or hatred against individuals or groups.

### Spam & Abuse
Automated or mass creation of QR codes for spamming, harassment, or other abusive purposes.
